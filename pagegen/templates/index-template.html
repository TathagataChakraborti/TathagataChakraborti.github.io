The International Conference on Automated Planning and Scheduling (ICAPS) is the premier conference on enabling smart decision-making in autonomous systems. ICAPS 2019 will be held July 11-15 in Berkeley (CA), USA.

ICAPS 2019 will be co-located with the 11th International Workshop on Planning and Scheduling for Space (IWPSS 2019).
ICAPS 2019 is also nearby and immediately followed by the 12th Annual Symposium on Combinatorial Search (SoCS) in Napa, California from July 16-17; and the Summer School in Cognitive Robotics at the University of Southern California (USC) from July 17-21.

    To Fellow Planners,

    The ICAPS 2019 Organizing Team would like to invite you to submit a summary of your work featured in a past ICAPS conference for potential inclusion in a highlight feature on the ICAPS 2019 website. This segment of the website will serve to make the conference more accessible to AI researchers outside the planning community as well as to website visitors who may be new to AI and are curious about the kind of research that ICAPS engenders.

    We are particularly looking for high impact works (including, but not limited to, contributions that have been awarded Best/Classic Papers, Dissertations, Demos, etc.) that have appeared in previous ICAPS conferences. If you are interested, please contact

    -- tchakra2 [AT] asu [DOT] edu

    with a brief abstract (~100 words) of your contribution and 1 to 3 1000x600 images that you think best illustrates your work. The latter can be a snapshot of working software, deployment, etc. (and usually not architecture diagrams, pseudocodes or algorithm walkthroughs). We are looking for illustrations that would be visually appealing and of interest to persons who are interested in AI but may not be aware of the role of planning and scheduling.
