  1. Request Visa Invitation Letter

In order to obtain an invitation letter from ICAPS 2019, invited speakers and authors of papers can send an email to

  • siddharths [AT] asu.edu with "ICAPS visa invitation letter" in the subject
  • with the following information: name, address, affiliation, paper title, nationality and date of birth. If you do not have a paper in the main conference, please mention your workshop, tutorial or demo track involvement.
The invitation letter can be used for visa applications. The letter cannot be used for refunds on registration fees.
