About ICAPS

The International Conference on Automated Planning and Scheduling (ICAPS) is the premier forum for researchers and practitioners in planning and scheduling - two technologies that are critical to manufacturing, space systems, software engineering, robotics, education, and entertainment. The ICAPS conference resulted from merging two bi-annual conferences, namely the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence Planning and Scheduling (AIPS) and the European Conference on Planning (ECP).

ICAPS provides student support partly covering travel and/or registration costs thanks to our sponsors. The exact amount depends on the funding from sponsors, your location, and the number of other applicants.

  • Please apply for student support by asking your supervisor, or the school administration to send a letter (in PDF, using official letterhead) directly to icaps.dc.2019@gmail.com verifying the applicantâs enrolment in a PhD program.
  • The deadline for applying is April 8, 2019. Notification is April 20.

Registering for the Doctoral Consortium will be part of the registration process for the conference.
